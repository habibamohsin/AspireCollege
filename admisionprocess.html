Eligibility Criteria

Aspire College ensures a clear, transparent and student-friendly admission system where every applicant is guided step by step.

verified General Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility ye hai ke student ka previous degree/qualification clear hona zaroori hai taake woh next academic level main enrollment se pehle properly eligible ho. Yeh rule har program ke liye basic requirement hai jisse academic continuity maintain rahe.

school Intermediate Programs Admission

Intermediate programs (FSc, ICS, ICom, Arts) ke liye students ka minimum 45% marks in Matric hona zaroori hai. Admissions merit aur seat availability ke basis par finalize kiye jate hain. College students ko supportive learning environment aur experienced faculty provide karta hai.

account_balance BS / University Programs Admission

BS programs ke liye candidates ka Intermediate complete hona required hai. University programs main eligibility academic performance aur university guidelines ke mutabiq check ki jati hai. Har student ko proper counseling di jati hai taake woh apne career ke liye best program choose kar sake.

assignment Admission Process

Admission process simple aur step-by-step hai:
• Application submit karna
• Required documents provide karna
• Verification process complete karna
• Final approval ke baad enrollment confirm hota hai

Is complete process ke dauran students ko admission office full guidance provide karta hai.

star Our Commitment

Aspire College merit-based admissions, transparency, aur equal opportunity par yaqeen rakhta hai. Hamara goal har student ko quality education aur successful future ki taraf guide karna hai.
